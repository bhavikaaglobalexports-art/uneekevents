✦ Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with UNEEK EVENTS UK.

How do I book your services?
+
Simply use our Contact page or call us directly to arrange a free initial consultation. During this meeting we'll discuss your vision, date, budget and requirements. Once we're aligned, we'll send you a detailed proposal and event agreement to formalise the booking.
What is your pricing structure?
+
Every event is unique, so we provide bespoke quotes based on your specific requirements. Our fees typically cover a management retainer plus supplier coordination costs. We're always transparent — no hidden charges. Contact us for a tailored quote and we'll work within your budget wherever possible.
How far in advance should I book?
+
We recommend booking at least 9–12 months in advance for weddings and major corporate events, and 3–6 months for smaller occasions. That said, we have successfully delivered events with much shorter lead times — please don't hesitate to reach out even for last-minute enquiries.
Do you work outside of the UK?
+
Our primary focus is the United Kingdom, but we do manage destination events in Europe for existing clients. If you're interested in an event abroad, please contact us and we'll discuss the feasibility based on your dates and location.
Can you accommodate specific cultural or religious requirements?
+
Absolutely. We are deeply experienced in planning events that honour a wide range of cultural, religious and dietary requirements — including South Asian, Middle Eastern, African, and Western traditions. Inclusivity and sensitivity are central to how we work.
What happens if something goes wrong on the day?
+
Our on-the-day coordination team handles every contingency so you never have to worry. We have robust contingency plans for weather, supplier no-shows, technical issues and more. All our events are covered by our comprehensive liability insurance.
Do you provide a day-of coordination service only?
+
Yes — in addition to full event management, we offer a Day-of Coordination package for clients who have planned their own event but want professional oversight on the day. Contact us to learn more about this service.
Are you insured?
+
Yes. UNEEK EVENTS UK holds full public liability insurance and professional indemnity insurance. Documentation can be provided to venues upon request as part of our standard event preparation process.

Still have questions? We'd love to hear from you.
